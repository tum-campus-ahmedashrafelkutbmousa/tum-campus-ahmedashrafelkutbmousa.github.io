By entering the key and pin of the document presented to you, a digital copy of the document can be opened here.

The authenticity of the document is verified by the fact that you open it on the website of the issuing institution. Therefore, you should carefully examine the URL of the website displaying the digital copy.

Please note that failure of verification does not mean that the document is a forgery. It may mean there are technical problems, verification may have been disabled for the document, or it may have expired. If necessary, request a new document to be issued.

If you have any questions, please contact our IT-Support.

Data Protection: Storage, duplication, disclosure or processing of the document or data obtained from the document beyond that granted to you for the purpose of processing the document originally issued is not permitted. Stored documents must be deleted immediately if storage is not explicitly authorized.
